## Problem Statement:
### Small businesses represent the majority of global companies but face uncertain growth trajectories. This project aims to predict the growth potential of small and mid-sized companies by analyzing historical firmographic data.

## Goal:
### Identify which companies are most likely to enter a high-growth phase (e.g., significant increase in employee count or market expansion) within the next few years based on their characteristics and industry trends.
